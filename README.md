# Futureminds
Future Minds is a free, interactive learning app for K–5 students featuring quizzes, reading stories, vocabulary builders, a math bubble game, and a customizable avatar system. This takes place in an all in one beautifully themed, no-login-required experience.

---

##  Live App

 **[Launch Future Minds](https://kpranger19-beep.github.io/Futureminds/)**

Works on any device — desktop, tablet, or phone. No account needed.

---

##  Overview

Future Minds is a fully self-contained educational web app designed for students in Kindergarten through 5th grade. It covers core subjects like Math, Reading, Science, and Social Studies through interactive quizzes, curated video lessons, guided stories, vocabulary practice, and a math arcade game — all wrapped in a beautifully themed, gamified experience that keeps kids engaged and coming back.

Everything runs in the browser. No backend, no server, no logins — just open the link and start learning.

---

##  Features

###  Practice & Quizzes
- Multi-subject quizzes across Math, Reading, Science, and Social Studies
- Grade-adaptive questions for K–5
- Instant feedback with explanations
- Stars and XP earned for every correct answer

### Reading Stories
- Leveled reading passages organized by grade
- Comprehension-focused content with vocabulary highlights
- Supports independent reading and guided learning

###  Vocabulary Builder
- Grade-specific word lists with definitions, examples, and syllable breakdowns
- Built-in text-to-speech so students can hear every word pronounced
- Memory tips and usage examples for each word

###  Math Bubble Game
- Fast-paced arcade game where players pop the bubble with the correct answer
- Problems use addition, subtraction, and multiplication
- Speeds up as your score grows — great for math fluency practice
- Unlocks after earning 15 

###  Video Lessons
- Curated educational videos organized by subject and grade
- Embedded YouTube player built right into the app

###  Star Store & Rewards
- Earn stars by completing quizzes and games
- Spend stars on avatars, accessories, and power-ups in the in-app store
- Encourages consistent practice through a reward loop

###  Avatar & Character Builder
- Fully customizable character with face, body, and accessory options
- Set your character as your profile avatar

###  Badges & Achievements
- Earn badges for milestones like quiz streaks, high scores, and reading goals
- Tracked on your personal profile page

4. Start learning and earning stars!

##  Tech Stack

- **Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** — zero frameworks, zero dependencies
- **localStorage** for saving progress locally in the browser
- **Web Speech API** for text-to-speech in the Vocabulary section
- Self-contained in a single `.html` file
